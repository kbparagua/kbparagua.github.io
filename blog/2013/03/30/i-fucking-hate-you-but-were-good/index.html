I Fucking Hate You! But We're Good.

Mar 30th, 2013 | Comments

I have a terrible secret; I sucked at socialization. And I sucked at grammar. Is it really right that I used semicolon there? Is it okay that I started a sentence with an ‘And’?

Yes, I can talk and hangout with people. But when he/she says or do something bullshit, well fuck, it will be over. That will be the final friendly activity the two of us will have. All remaining encounters will be small hi-hello conversations. I’m just so allergic to bullshits, attention whores, know-it-all guys, and all those fucking annoying people I encounter everyday.

Then recently, I asked myself a stupid question: Hey, why do you have too few friends? I mean really close friends.

Seriously that is a stupid thing to ask. Obviously It’s because you fucking hate everybody. I fucking hate everyone.

But, no matter how much I want to kill someone, I really feel sad. Why? Come on, that’s a stupid question.

I’m an avid fan of Disney’s Penis and Perv, something that sounds like that. I particularly want the scenes where Dr. Doofenshmirtz, the main antagonist, tells his backstory which explains and justifies his present evil plans.

Hmm… So?

Well, I think we all have an intense story behind our bullshit behavior. You’ve been bullied during high school? Your parents think you’re a big disappointment? We all have this insane traumatic story that make this present us imperfect. Imperfect to be liked by everybody.

And I’ve denied that fact for years, thinking that people are just fucking annoying because they are born that way.

Everyone. I’m sorry. I’m also full of bullshit. I’m no different. We are all full of bullshits. You know what I’ve realized: I need bullshits in my life. We need bullshits to complete our life.

Happiness is not from a perfect circle of friends. It is from a shitty circle of friends occasionally throwing shits to each other then wiping out each other’s shit stains after.

Anyway, I still hate you. But we can talk and have some beer sometime. :)
